Getting Here

For those flying in, the Philadelphia Intl Airport (PHL) is the closest airport, which offers public transportation and private taxis into the city.


The wedding will be located in Northeast Philadelphia, approximately a 30 minute drive from the airport via I-95.

Where to Stay

We will arrange a room block for guests traveling from out of town. More details will be provided at a later date.

Please note - rooms are only available through our room block for the following nights:
Friday, April 18th
Saturday, April 19th

Getting Around

We'll be coordinating transportation from the hotel to Glen Foerd, but for when you want to get out and explore the city yourself, there are plenty of ways to get around.

Car

For out of town guests, a car will not be necessary to travel between the hotel and venue as transportation will be provided to and from the hotel to the venue. For guests who reside locally and guests who wish to rent a car, there will be valet parking available at Glen Foerd Mansion.

Uber & Lyft

Uber tends to be our app-of-choice, but Lyft also has good coverage as well.
